    Hello Neighbor Wiki Official Hello Neighbor Song

    DevGam to Prototype

    The DevGam was the first ever model of the game. Nobody
    knew it existed until 2018 when TinyBuild Games announced it.
    The Prototype was annouced in 2016 as the first trailer for the game.
    You can actually play it if your Hello Neighbor game allows mods.

    Act 1

    Act 1 was the first act of Hello Neighbor. It featured Nicky Roth (Player) as a kid. He
    has to sneak in to Theodore Peterson's (Neighbor) basement. They way to
    do it is to grab cardboard boxes at the back of the house.

    Act 2

    Act 2 is the second act of Hello Neighbor. Act 2 is the complete opposite of Act 1. In
    Act 2 you have to escape the neighbor's house by choping the fence with a crowbar,
    getting a key, or jumping on a trampoline.
