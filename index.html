    DevGam to Prototype

    The DevGam was the first ever model of the game. Nobody
    knew it existed until 2018 when TinyBuild Games announced it.
    The Prototype was annouced in 2016 as the first trailer for the game.
    You can actually play it if your Hello Neighbor game allows mods.

    The Hello Neighbor Pre-Alpha was the first model people could play.
    Both the Pre-Alpha and Alpha 1 had a nighmare. In the Pre-Alpha use could use the nighmare for a glitch by getting a trash can from the neighbor's house and then put it on the players bed. In Hello Neighbor Alpha 1 gave us some more info about why we were sneaking in to the neighbor's house. Alpha 1.5 was never released so we don't know anything about it. Alpha 2 was a huge update!!! How Huge you ask? Well it eventually became Act 1 one of Hello Neighbor. Alpha 3 was another huge update because it became Act 3. Alpha 4 was the same as Alpha 3 except it became beta 2.
